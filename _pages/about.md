Hello! I am an incoming MSc student in Statistical Science at the [University of Oxford](https://www.ox.ac.uk/). Prior to this, I obtained my B.S. in Statistics with highest distinction (4.0/4.0) from [UNC Chapel Hill](https://www.unc.edu/). 

My academic and research interests are rooted in my statistical training and hands-on modeling experiences. Drawing from my previous lab work and coursework, I am particularly interested in **Bayesian Inference, Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC), and Machine Learning**. I enjoy applying these computational and statistical methods to solve practical, interdisciplinary challenges, especially in areas like **computational biology** and **data analysis**.

Outside of research and coding, I am a huge football fan and proudly support **Manchester United**. In my free time, you can also find me playing the piano or watching anime.
